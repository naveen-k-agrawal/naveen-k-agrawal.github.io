Naveen Kumar Agrawal

PhD Researcher · KTH Royal Institute of Technology

Rheology · Fluid Mechanics · CFD · Soft Matter · Micro-swimmers

I am a PhD researcher at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm. My research focuses on the mechanics of complex fluids, including rheology, fluid mechanics, and CFD, with applications to soft matter and micro-swimmers.

I study phase transitions and collective behavior in active-passive particle systems, dense suspension dynamics, and the design of micro-robots for biomedical applications. [Add your undergraduate background and any internships here.]
